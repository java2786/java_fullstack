BMI Calculator
    BMI = Weight (kg) / (Height (m) * Height (m)).
        Categorize the BMI result as:
        Underweight: BMI < 18.5
        Normal: 18.5 <= BMI < 25
        Overweight: 25 <= BMI < 30
        Obese: BMI >= 30
Example:
    Input: Weight = 70, Height = 1.75
    Output: BMI = 22.86 (Normal)
